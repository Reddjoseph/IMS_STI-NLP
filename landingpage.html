Systems and Technology Institute
(STI Dasmariñas)

Systems and Technology Institute (STI) – Dasmariñas is one of the leading educational institutions in Cavite, providing top-notch education in Information Technology, Engineering, Business, and allied programs. With a strong commitment to excellence and innovation, STI Dasmariñas prepares students to become competent and globally competitive professionals.

Purpose of the Inventory System

The STI Dasmariñas Inventory System streamlines the management of all laboratory equipment, tools, and resources. It ensures accurate tracking, organized maintenance scheduling, and efficient resource monitoring. This digital approach minimizes errors, enhances accountability, and promotes a sustainable and transparent operational workflow within the institution.
